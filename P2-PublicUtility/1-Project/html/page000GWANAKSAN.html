The Gymnasium

When : All days 09:00 - 22:00. However if you want to play on a court you have to reserve it first.

Where : Builidng 71 and 71-1

What to bring : Nothing to bring

What to do : The 71 Building is actually the old gym. It's got this massive area with three courts right next to each other. You can play badminton, volleyball, handball, and basketball there, but you have to join a club or book the fields. Upstairs, they've got judo, taekwondo, and table tennis. Oh, and on the first floor, there's a fitness gym too. You can just walk in without booking, but it's pretty small and doesn't have a ton of machines. Now, in the new gym, called the 71-1 building, there's this big gymnasium where you can play volleyball, handball, badminton, and basketball too. Behind the 71 Gymnasium, there's a swimming pool in the 71-2 building. I haven't tried it yet, though, but heart it is good. Oh, and they've got soccer, tennis, and baseball fields too.
