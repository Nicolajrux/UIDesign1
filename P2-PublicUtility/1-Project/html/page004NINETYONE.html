The Secret Study room

When : Only Weekdays 09:00 - 19:00 o clock.

Where : At building 14. The main door usually is locked, you have to go around it and enter from the side

What to bring : Bring your Student ID card or alternativly download and login to the MySNU app. Sometimes you need your id card or the QR code of the app to enter the room

What to do : It is agood place for studying. Close to the dorms, and not many people know about it. The chairs are super comfy, and they even have a whiteboard with pens.

Next Magic Word : Inside of the room you find a green painting. The next Magic Word is the name of the painter of the painting - 5 letters all caps no spacing nor extra characters.
